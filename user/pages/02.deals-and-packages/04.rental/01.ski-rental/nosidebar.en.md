#Renting Ski Gear in Gulmarg
You may not feel like lugging all your ski gear on your long plane ride to Ski Gulmarg or maybe you don't even have your own ski gear. It is possible to rent ski gear in Gulmarg…

###What to expect when you rent Ski Gear in Gulmarg.
Gulmarg and the ski rental facilities in Gulmarg are still far from the western standards.

Yes, there is some very good gear in Gulmarg to be rented; one rental store is accociated with K2. So, it is possible to rent K2 skis: Pontoon, Hellbent, Sethback, etc.

Another rental shop bought lots of skis from tourists and also got a big supply from a Whistler rental shop.

Starting in 2013-2014, a tour company with whom we work with offers gear rental. They have excellent gear like Black Diamonds, Jones Snowboards, etc. As they had to pay lots of import taxes, it costs a fair amount to rent that type of new gear in Gulmarg.

What I mean when saying that the standard is different is that skis might not be tuned up to perfection, with a few core shots. There will not be 15 staff members to help you get your gear adjusted for you in the morning so you will have to wait and most likely wait some more.

###Ski Rental Prices:
It all depends on what you need and what you are willing to pay. Renting new rocker fat powder skis costs more than old carving skis.

Visit our [Rental Rates](../rental-rates) page to get a good Idea.

###Who we work with when Our Guests need to Rent Ski Gear
We work with all private ski & snowboard rental shops. It all depends on what you need and where the ski gear is available. There are lots of skis to rent but it can get tricky with poles, ski clothes, transceivers, probe, and shovels.
