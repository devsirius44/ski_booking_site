#Learn to Ski or Snowboard in Gulmarg

Dreaming about learning to ski or snowboard? We offer beginner packages to suit your needs. Choose one of our 3 hotels - Khyber Himalayan Resort & Spa, Hotel Highlands Park or Hotel Shaw Inn. We chose these hotels to maximize comfort and proximity to the beginner slopes. Pictures leaning to ski surrounded by the beautiful Himalayas.    

##Learning to Ski or Snowboard

You can come to Gulmarg to learn to ski or ride as a solo traveler, a group of 2, 3, 4 or as a family. For non-family, your group will have a private professional instructor. If you are more than 4, no problem, just book more than 1 group.

##Learning to Ski or Snowboard as a Family

Imagine learning to ski or snowboard with your children! This is one of the most amazing mountain experiences for a parent. You have the chance to ski or ride with them, but you can also choose a package to fit your family's needs.

As a family a private lesson is the best way to learn and enjoy your time in Gulmarg Ski Resort. We have packages for 3 people - 2 adults and 1 child or 2 adults and 3 children. 1, 2, 3 or 4 of you can be learning, you decide.

##Here are your choices

####Group Package
+ Solo Traveller - 1 room - 1 person - 1 on 1 with the instructor
+ 2 Riders - 1 room - 2 people with the instructor
+ 3 Riders - 1 room - 3 people with the instructor
+ 4 Riders - 2 rooms - 4 people with the instructor

####Family Package
+ Family of 3 - 1 room - 1 person skiing with the instructor.
+ Family of 3 - 1 room - 2 person skiing with the instructor.
+ Family of 3 - 1 room - 3 person skiing with the instructor.
+ 2 Adults - 2 children - 2 rooms -  1 person skiing with the instructor.
+ 2 Adults - 2 children - 2 rooms -  2 person skiing with the instructor.
+ 2 Adults - 2 children - 2 rooms -  3 person skiing with the instructor.
+ 2 Adults - 2 children - 2 rooms -  4 person skiing with the instructor.

You can add as many instructors as you may need to progress as fast as you may want.
